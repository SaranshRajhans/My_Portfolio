About Me

01

Hi, I'm Saransh Rajhans — curious, and currently figuring things out at Lissa School of Design in Bangalore (3rd year). I like to draw — always have — but these days, I'm especially into motion graphics and 2D animation.

I wouldn't call myself a specialist (yet), but I've got a little something going on in a lot of areas. Whether it's sketching random ideas, experimenting with new tools, or just getting lost in animation loops, I love the feeling of creating stuff that moves — literally.

I'm still exploring, still learning — and honestly, that's my favourite part.
